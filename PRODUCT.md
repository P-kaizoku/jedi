# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Job seekers, particularly developers and technical professionals, who need a high-density, low-friction tool to track their job application pipeline and keep notes on their progress.

## Product Purpose
Jedi exists to help candidates manage their active job search. It serves as a single source of truth for applications, interview stages, and notes, replacing scattered spreadsheets with an efficient dashboard.

## Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Technical, professional, precise
- **Tone**: Focused, expert, efficient
- **Aesthetic**: Clean developer-focused (sleek dark/light themes, high contrast, sharp borders, high information density)

## Anti-references
- Warm cream/beige default SaaS templates (monoculture AI styling)
- Oversized headings and bloated layouts with giant empty spaces
- Clunky nested cards and generic gradient kickers
- Low-contrast gray text on off-white backgrounds

## Design Principles
1. **Utility First**: Design for action. Status updates, URL links, and note-taking must be immediate, clickable, and clear.
2. **High Information Density**: Present job application status, dates, and note indicators in a compact, readable layout that maximizes screen real estate.
3. **Structured Hierarchy**: Use typographic contrast (weight and scale) and sharp borders rather than card wrappers or drop shadows to group information.
4. **Accessible Contrast**: Ensure all text elements—including placeholders and badges—have strong visual contrast meeting or exceeding WCAG AA standards.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios of ≥ 4.5:1 for body and supporting text.
- Full support for system-wide light/dark themes.
- Graceful degradation of animation/motion for users who prefer reduced motion.
